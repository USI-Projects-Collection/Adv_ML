\subsection{Discussion}

All our experiments confirm claims from the paper regarding the definition of outlier patches and their information content compared to normal patches. 

\subsubsection{What was easy}
The paper clearly describes the pipeline used to produce the claimed results, even though the code was not released.

\subsubsection{What was difficult}
For the first 3 experiments, the paper does not explicitly specify the datasets used; nevertheless, the results hold with the dataset we selected. For the last experiment across multiple datasets, it was impractical for us to run probes on the full size of each dataset. Fortunately, since ViT models often produce highly informative representations, probing on a portion of each dataset is enough to verify qualitatively the global properties of outlier patches.
